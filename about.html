About me

I'm a Linux user and FOSS enthusiast. I like to hack on a software to make my life easier, that's why my editor of choice is Emacs and my Linux distro is NixOS.

For my coding projects you can check out my projects page where I list all of my creations with some information about them. I tend to write my code in either Rust or Python but I sometimes like to dabble with something related to frontend dev like Next.js or Vue.

When it comes to my other hobbies I like to read manga and books about technology and Japan. I also really enjoy playing rhythm games such as Pump It Up, osu! and Etterna. You can find more information about my rhythm games contributions and other staff here.

I also own two cats.
