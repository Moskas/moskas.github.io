Yet another website revision

Posted on: 20.07.2023 - @Moskas

But still fully static, at least for now.

The more I look at other people's blogs and websites in general the more I want to create a minimal website. That's focused on plain text rather than fancy animations and images that distract you from the main content of the website/blog, that is text.

A screenshot of the old desing with bootstrap

Why static and no javascript?

Mainly because I really enjoy reading the thoughts and knowledge that people try to convey on their websites. I'm not looking for a flashy websites made with the latest frameworks.

Sometimes I use a simple web browser that doesn't support these features (like qutebrowser). I also like to read the posts from a terminal via RSS reader and some people just don't account that some things might not be parsed correctly from the RSS xml.

Thanks to that I would like to create a nicely looking website that's focused on plain text with small additions of images.

Why gruvbox colorscheme and single column design?

I just like how gruvboxs colors look in general. I use it throught my system, in terminal, webbrowser, text editor etc.

Single column because I find it much more readable and approachable when you have lots of text presented at once. Some people might think that just a waste of a horizontal space but in my opinion readability is more important than efficient screenspace usage. Additionaly the website basically looks the same on fullscreen and when tilled. Since I'm mostly tilling my windows and rarely have one window one a screen at a time it's pretty convinient.
